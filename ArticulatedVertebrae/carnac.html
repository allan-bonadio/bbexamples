Carnac the Magnificent riddles

Johnny Carson used to put on a turban and pretend to be a psychic by holding up an envelope to his head, and guessing the answer to the enclosed question (answers written on outside of envelope really so Johnny could read them). Many of the jokes were racist, sexist, US-centric, or xenophobic, but that was the audience in the 1980s. I'm Italian so I left one of those in.
